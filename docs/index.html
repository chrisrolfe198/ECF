The aim of this project

The aim of this project is to provide a structure similar to Bootstrap that allows you to quickly create HTML Email templates that work to a degree across clients.

With HTML email many developers will tell you that you need to first understand that emails will look different in each client and accepting that is the first step to creating an email that looks reasonable cross platform. To quote MailChimp

The most important thing (if you wish to preserve your sanity) is to keep it simple. Focus on your message, not your craftiness.

Taken from the MailChimp Blog
